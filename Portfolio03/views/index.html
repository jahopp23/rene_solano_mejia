About

I am a Full Stack Web Developer in Dallas, Texas. While studying for a B.A. in Anthropology at University of North Texas, I gained a true interest in computer science and software engineering. After graduating in 2014, I decided to blend my passion for art and science by purusing software engineering & web development full-time at Coding Dojo. I specialize in the design and construction of web applications: from conception to production. This includes creating mockups, building the server (local/cloud), hooking up synchronous databases, and design/programming for front-end interfaces...all while keeping in mind wonderful user experiences! I believe the web & software bring the world together with its capacity to connect people through challenging projects and collaboration of skills.
